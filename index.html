Lucky 9 Card Game

Rules and Objectives:

The objective of this game is to have a high total card value or closest to Nine as possible. Cards A,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 are all counted according to their numerical value. Cards 10, Jack, Queen, and King have no value. For example: 8 + 5 = 13 which counts as 3
